# Blinkit

### `Overview`
A complete data cleaning and analysis project performed using SQL and Power BI.
Data preprocessing, transformation, and DAX-based calculations were implemented to deliver insight-ready and interactive dashboards.

Dashboard Link : https://shorturl.at/r94k5

---

### `Separate reports were created for the following business segments:`
1. Customers
2. Orders
3. Products
4. Transaction
5. Refund
6. Feedback

---

Total Customers : 2000
Active Customer : 1823
Inactive customers : 177
Average Orders : 2.74
Average Age : 39.12
Repeat Customer rate : 78.44%
Total Revenue : 58539K
Average order value : 11.71K

---

### `Business Insights`
The 36–45 age group contributes the highest number of orders, followed by 26–35 years.
Noida, Pune, Kolkata, and Mumbai are the top-performing cities by customer volume.
The active customer rate is 91.5%, indicating strong retention.
Weekend orders make up 71.34% of total transactions, showing higher engagement during weekends.
Top 5 high-paying products:
1. Mi Power Bank Air
2. Samsung Earbuds
3. Sony Camera Fit
4. Realme Power Bank
5. Redragon Keyboard

---

### `Opportunities Identified`
1. Focus marketing campaigns on 26–45 age group across top-performing cities.
2. Leverage weekend promotions to further boost sales
3. Optimize inventory and recommendations for top-performing product categories.
